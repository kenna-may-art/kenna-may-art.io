PROJECT GOAL

My goal was to build a visually cohesive game demo with a mysterious industrial theme with bio-mechanical components. Because I was working independently, I developed a reusable production pipeline for character sprites, environments, interface assets, and narrative implementation.

KEY RESULTS

  • Released a playable public demo
  • 50+ early downloads
  • 5+ five star ratings
  • Received paid player contribution
  • Ongoing development based on player feedback

WHAT I LEARNED

Making Mechanical Heart taught me how to maintain visual consistency across characters, environments, and UI while managing the scope of an independent production. It challenged me to balance my creative side with the practicalities of a solo development process.

I learned to prototype, revise, and adapt. All to deliver an enjoyable experience.
