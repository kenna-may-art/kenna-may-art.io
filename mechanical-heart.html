PROJECT GOAL

My goal was to build a visually cohesive narrative-game demo with a mysterious gothic atmosphere. Because I was working independently, I developed a reusable production pipeline for character sprites, environments, interface assets, and narrative implementation.

CHARACTER DEVELOPMENT

CASTER PROTO COLLECTOR

Caster's silhouette balances theatrical authority with visible fragility. The structured coat and mask communicate control, while the damaged mechanical details hint that the performance is unstable.

ENVIRONMENT DEVELOPMENT

THE CLOCK SHOP

The shop needed to feel cluttered and historic without interfering with dialogue readability. I reserved low-contrast areas behind the character sprites and concentrated important props around exploration points.
