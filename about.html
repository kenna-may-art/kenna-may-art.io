ABOUT ME

MULTIMEDIA DESIGNER & GAME ARTIST

I am Kenna May, a multimedia designer who enjoys building visual identities for story-driven projects. My work blends illustration, UI design, and motion to create cohesive game experiences from concept through final polish.

I especially enjoy designing characters and interfaces that support worldbuilding, emotion, and gameplay clarity. Whether I am creating key art, in-game assets, or promotional visuals, I focus on readability, atmosphere, and personality.

  • Character concepting and visual development
  • UI design for menus, HUD systems, and narrative screens
  • Motion graphics and polished presentation assets
